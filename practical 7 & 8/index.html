Using Special Characters in a Web Page

Here are some commonly used special characters:

  • Ampersand: &
  • Less than: <
  • Greater than: >
  • Double Quote: "
  • Single Quote (Apostrophe): '
  • Copyright Symbol: ©
  • Registered Trademark: ®
  • Euro Sign: €
  • Trademark Symbol: ™
  • Non-breaking Space:  
  • Check Mark: ✓
  • Heart Symbol: ♥

Mathematical symbols:

  • Multiplication Sign: ×
  • Division Sign: ÷
  • Plus-Minus Sign: ±
  • Less Than or Equal To: ≤
  • Greater Than or Equal To: ≥
  • Pi Symbol: π
  • Infinity: ∞
